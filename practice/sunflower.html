The Sunflower Friend

Once upon a time, there was a little girl named Lily. She had a unique friend, a sunflower. Every day, Lily would talk to the sunflower and share her dreams.
One day, the sunflower seemed to be drooping. Lily was worried. She gently watered it and said, "You are my best friend, and I will always take care of you."

A Special Bond

Lily and the sunflower grew together. The sunflower always stood tall, and Lily felt the magic of care and friendship.
